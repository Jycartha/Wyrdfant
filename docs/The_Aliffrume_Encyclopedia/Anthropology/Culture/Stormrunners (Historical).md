# Stormrunners (Historical)
A major component of Áren culture, equivalent to mythological heroes. Against the stoneghost anti-firekeeping doctrine, they were notable for using firekeeping to maneuver the storms without relying on infrastructure, expelling the colonizers and making firekeeping a symbol of national pride.  

Though historically the Stormrunners were [soulkeepers](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Applied%20Phlogistics/Soulkeeping.md), time has changed them to also be thought of as gimyndine wielders, subsequently legitimizing the practice.
