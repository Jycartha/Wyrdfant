### Powderstaff

A common implement for support-focused or civilian [flamebearers](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Firekeeping/Flamebearer.md), and a catch-all term for any device which constructs a [monocrystal](../Materials/Pneumaphilics/Adamant.md) tool. In this case, monocrystal’s use lies within its reusability and relatively high [soulfire](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Soulfire.md) capacity. Some [engrams](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Engram.md) require intense bursts of gimynfire, but uncoupled gimynfire disperses quickly, and the human body will succumb to [[glassrot]] rapidly if [coupling](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Interactions/Coupling.md) is attempted. As a solution, many tools form monocrystals as a form of quick-discharge capacitor, often resembling staffs or floating orbs.
