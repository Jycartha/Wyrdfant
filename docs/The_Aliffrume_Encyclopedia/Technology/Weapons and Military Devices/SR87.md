### SR87

The standard-issue [flamebearer](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Flamebearer.md)'s scepter of Saorann, which is also able to transform into a sniper rifle through the use of a simple [Engram](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Engram.md). Can house up to 20 [kindlegems](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Technology/Kindlegem.md), most often long-range support ones. Common engrams include reflective shields, [Gimyndine](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Gimyndine.md) resonator bombs, aerial bombardment, and trap fields.
