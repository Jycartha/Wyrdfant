# Divinity
A divinity is a specific classification of [engram](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Engram.md) which provides for the construction of an [ichorous](../../Glossary.md#Ichorous) artifact; typically a body part, but may also be an external tool. 

Given their nature, divinities are chiefly of interest to high-level [firekeepers](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Firekeeping/Firekeeper.md), and those who are interested in having something to offer them.
