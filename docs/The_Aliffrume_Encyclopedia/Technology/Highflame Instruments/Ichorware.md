# Ichorware
A specific classification of [engram](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Engram.md) which provides for the construction of an [ichorous](../../Glossary.md#Ichorous) artifact; typically a body part, but may also be an external tool. The term "ichorware" may be used to refer to either the engram detailing the construction of the artifact, or the artifact itself.

Given its nature, ichorware is chiefly of interest to high-level [firekeepers](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Firekeeping/Firekeeper.md), and those who are interested in having something to offer them.
