# Blackfire Device
A blackfire device is any instrument crafted with the use of low-grade [gimpowder](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Gimyndine/Gimyndine.md#Ashstone) in mind, with highly efficient [Flamescript](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Flamescript.md) engraved into it. Due to the extremely low [gimynfire](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Soul/Soulfire.md#Gimynfire) production of [blackstone](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Gimyndine/Gimyndine.md#Ashstone)–even when combusted–the flamescript which shapes the functions of these devices must be equally efficient, requiring a highly skilled [flamescribe](../../Glossary.md#Flamescribe). Due to this limitation, [Firekeeping](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Applied%20Phlogistics/Firekeeping.md) in the hands of the common folk is limited mostly to combustion, but many flamescribes and noble houses maintain similar items for convenience or war making, which are documented here.
