### Lightclock

One of the most common devices flamescribes produce for themselves, it a two-part lantern and timekeeping device. Constructed like an hourglass, [gimpowder](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Gimyndine.md#Ashstone) gradually falls down into an ongoing [Engram](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Engram.md) of a warm light and gets consumed. For [artificers](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Applied%20Phlogistics/Artifice.md), this is often one of the first devices they make, as a good combination of physical mechanics and flamescript. These objects thus tend to have special import to their creators, and are decorated accordingly.
