### Dreamleaf Juice

A product drawn from the leaves of dreamleaf plants in the mist-forests of southern [Tohnipras](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Geography/Tohnipras.md), known for its unique psychedelic effect of [immersing](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/The%20Phlogistic%20Model.md#Immersionists) users in the thoughts and dreams had by others near the plant before the time of harvesting. This property is believed to be derived from the slight [pneumaphilic](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Technology/Materials/Pneumaphilics/Pneumaphilic%20Materials.md) properties of Tohneasian mist, carrying residual [Soulfire](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Soulfire.md) from the various creatures it contacts.  

Due to the especially unique quality of offering an exotic perspective, the juice is often vaporized and inhaled as a recreational drug by [Yejian](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Natural%20Sciences/Biology/Sapient%20Species/Llantakúna.md#Yejian) aristocrats, considered a high-class treat. The native [Ayauitlaka](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Natural%20Sciences/Biology/Sapient%20Species/Llantakúna.md#Ayauitlaka), however, consider the juice sacred as part of local tradition, used only during meditative rituals for the purposes of harmonizing with the forest. Using it for pleasure is considered blasphemous.
