### Goldwood
A semi-uncommon flowering tree native to the copperleaf forests around the Assurian Crater. It serves as the [Gimyndine](../../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Gimyndine.md) purifier for the ecosystem, housing a varied ecology of pneumivorous bacteria and an expansive rhizome root system. Purified [gimynfire](../../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Soulfire.md) precipitates out of the roots and into the soil of the forest, favoring near-to-ground plants and coating the forest in a blanket of sky-blue ivy and golden-white fog-like [Soulfire](../../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Soulfire.md).

Paper made from thin slices of goldwood is naturally infused, and thus noted for its ability to hold [Flamescript](../../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Flamescript.md) indefinitely. It is used widely in the art of intuitive storytelling practiced by the native [Nebvoltsi](../Sapient%20Species/Llantakúna.md#Nebvoltsi) and [Ayauitlaka](../Sapient%20Species/Llantakúna.md#Ayauitlaka), and remains a major export to foreign [firekeepers](../../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Firekeeper.md) without [kindlegems](../../../Technology/Kindlegem.md) or having traditional tastes.
