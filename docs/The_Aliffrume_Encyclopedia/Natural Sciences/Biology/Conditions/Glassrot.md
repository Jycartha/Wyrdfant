# Glassrot
A condition resulting from the [gimyndization](../../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Gimyndine/Gimyndine.md#Gimyndization) of organic matter, typically concurring as a result of overuse of ingestion of [gimyndine](../../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Gimyndine/Gimyndine.md). Due to its inherently phlogistic nature, glassrot is particularly difficult to heal through [soul assertion](../../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Soul%20Assertion.md), even if the affected area is removed, as the gimyndization process affects the [soul wall](../../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Soul/Soul.md#Soul%20Wall).
