### Shimmer-beasts
The name for a large group of predators native to copperleaf forests, which are capable of harnessing ambient [soulfire](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Soulfire.md) for their own [engrams](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Engram.md), and which typically possesses a [flamescript](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Flamescript.md)-like intuitive iridescence, from which they get their name. They are the only natural predators of the [Kaithurhi](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Natural%20Sciences/Biology/Sapient%20Species/Kuthjïuman.md#Kaithurhi), and are very difficult to defeat without adequate preparation.
