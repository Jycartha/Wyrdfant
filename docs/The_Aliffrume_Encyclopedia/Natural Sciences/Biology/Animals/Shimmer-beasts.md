### Shimmer-beasts
The name for a large group of predators native to copperleaf forests, which are capable of harnessing ambient [soulfire](../../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Soul/Soulfire.md) for their own [engrams](../../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Engram.md), and which typically possesses a [flamescript](../../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Flamescript.md)-like intuitive iridescence, from which they get their name. They are the only natural predators of the [Kaithurhi](../Sapient%20Species/Kuthjïuman.md#Kaithurhi), and are very difficult to defeat without adequate preparation.
