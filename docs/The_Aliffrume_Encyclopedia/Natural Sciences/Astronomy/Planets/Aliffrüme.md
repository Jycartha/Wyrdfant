## Moons
Aliffrüme has one stable moon with a mass of 1.1E+23 kg, which is dyed <span style="color:#00ffca">bluish-green</span> and <span style="color:#980000">rust red</span> with copper oxides, water ice, and nickel salts. On the whole, it has a roughly teal shine at night, with notable seas of red around active volcanoes.
