# Reinbrüne
The only sun of Aliffrüme’s system, a blue-white F-type main-sequence star. Notably, current estimates of the age of Aliffrüme by certain specimens place the planet at around 5 billion years old, a margin longer than the expected lifespan of such a star. This contradiction is a current focus of astronomical study.
