# Witafjürgen University
An institution nearly as old as Jïumannes itself, Witafjürgen University is an institution of higher learning and professional association located within the holy mountains of Tuihorm established by Älwathend Leombjürna Witafjürgen (his surname granted for its founding). In keeping with the meaning of its name, a “Forest of the Wise,” Witafjürgen’s principal purpose is as a professional group of [firekeepers](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Firekeeping/Firekeeper.md) and academics, which has a secondary purpose of teaching and screening potential new members of the group.

Witafjürgen is composed of six individual colleges, each dedicated to one area of research in [phlogistics](../../Glossary.md#Phlogistics), and founded a member of Leombjürna’s inner circle. Each college includes and manages their own undergraduate and graduate education, research, and professional syndicates. The main connecting line is their organizational structure, with each college being led by a master and their head professors (“tenures” in the vernacular), further subordinated by regular professors. The master of each college then serves on a board, which meets with the Grandmaster to decide on matters relating to the whole university.

In no particular order, the colleges include the: 
* Leombjürna College of [Noelogists](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Applied%20Phlogistics/Noelogy.md)
* Nichtfryth College of [Soulsmiths](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Applied%20Phlogistics/Soulsmithing.md)
* Skógrekin College of [Ichorcraft](../../Glossary.md#Ichorcraft) 
* Tdahiya College of [Computers](../../Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Applied%20Phlogistics/Phlogistic%20Computing.md)  
* Krifcalon College of Engineers  
* Eglavoé College of [Synthesizers](../../Anthropology/Economics/Synthesizer.md)
