# Flamescribe

Specifically, a [Firekeeper](Firekeeper.md) who specializes in the creation and transcription of [engrams](Pure%20Phlogistics/Engram.md). When used as a title, often shortened simply to "Scribe" or "Scr." in writing. 

Occasionally, one who studies new applications of [Firekeeping](Applied%20Phlogistics/Firekeeping.md). (overlap with [Phlogistician](Pure%20Phlogistics/Phlogistician.md))
