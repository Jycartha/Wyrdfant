# Noelogy
<!-- Derived from the greek root of νόος, meaning something like "mind," thus backforming to something like "the study of the mind" -->
The study of [soulfire](../Pure%20Phlogistics/Soul/Soulfire.md) and the [conscious plane](../Pure%20Phlogistics/Conscious%20Plane/Conscious%20Plane.md), and how soulfire interacts with itself. It involves the study of [infusion](../Soulfire/Interactions/Infusion.md) and the other fundamental interactions, [phlogistic computing](Phlogistic%20Computing.md), resonance, [gimynfire](../Pure%20Phlogistics/Soul/Soulfire.md#gimynfire) control, and other fields. Essentially, it is to firekeeping as physics is to engineering, and together with [firekeeping](Firekeeping.md) composes the wider field of [phlogistics](../../Glossary.md#Phlogistics).
