# Firekeeping

Applied [Phlogistics](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Phlogistics.md) for various purposes, including engineering, medicine, and many other fields.

---
## Usage

All firekeeping begins with the generation of [Soulfire](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Soulfire.md), which is then manipulated in some manner to achieve some effect. It can be suddenly compressed to exert an explosive pressure, run over [Flamescript](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Flamescript.md) to affect [engrams](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Engram.md), or used for its [fluid mechanics](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/The%20Phlogistic%20Model.md#Fluidic%20Pressure), in increasing order of complexity.

All life can control ambient [Soulfire](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Soulfire.md) and even produce its own, but sourcing it from [Gimyndine](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Gimyndine.md) is much more potent and allows easier use when integrated with [Kindlegem](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Technology/Kindlegem.md). This led to [Gimyndine](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Gimyndine.md) technology and its derivatives becoming the forefront of development for centuries, and many self-sourced firekeeping techniques became relegated to folk contexts or small communities, where the usage of [Gimyndine](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Gimyndine.md) was either impractical from extenuating circumstance, or rejected on principle.
