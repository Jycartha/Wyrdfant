### Flamebearer  
*(Also referred to as scriptlighters)*  
Specifically, a [Firekeeper](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Firekeeper.md) who specializes in implementing and utilizing engrams. Typically shortened to "lighter" outside of documents or ritual procedures. Shortened in writing to "Ltr."
