# Firekeeper  
One who practices [Firekeeping](../Applied%20Phlogistics/Firekeeping.md), including the creation of [Engram](../Pure%20Phlogistics/Engram.md) or their implementation, medicine through assisted assertion, or any other such broad fields. Shortened to "Kpr." in writing.

## Gimynkeeper
A [Firekeeper](Firekeeper.md) who primarily utilizes a gimyn as their source of intention.
