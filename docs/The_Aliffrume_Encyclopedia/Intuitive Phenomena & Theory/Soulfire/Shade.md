# Shade
A kind of malicious [phantasm](Phantasm.md) constituted by chaotic thoughts, feelings, and emotions. 

In the case of phlogenic shades, they are typically able to sustain themselves over extremely wide dominions, as their source is often the impression generated by dense life around them. As such, shades grow in density and power within the deep forest, marshes, grasslands, and other areas of dense wilderness.
