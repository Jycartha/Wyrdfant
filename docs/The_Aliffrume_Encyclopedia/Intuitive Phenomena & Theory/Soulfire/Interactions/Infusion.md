### Infusion

Perhaps the most basic interaction, [Soulfire](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Soulfire.md) undergoes infusion when it’s radially compressed by [the Conscious Plane](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Conscious%20Plane.md), shunting it down into the physical realm. This process also generates a form of radiation called [sparks](/The_Aliffrume_Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Soulfire.md#Sparks), in order to preserve the information lost in converting continuous soulfire to quantized reality. Only infused soulfire is capable of directly interacting with matter or the physical forces.
