### Artifice

The study of combining [Flamescript](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Flamescript.md) with machinery, giving rise to contraptions capable of [Firekeeping](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Applied%20Phlogistics/Firekeeping.md). The field is further broken down into fields normally associated with purely physical engineering: Aerial artifice, mechanical artifice, etc. The distinction is practical as not all environments are as conducive to [Gimyndine](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Gimyndine.md) as others, and some applications simply don’t merit the complexity of phlogistic techniques.
