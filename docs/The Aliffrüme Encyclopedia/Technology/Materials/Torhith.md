### Torhith

A mineral exclusively found in the steamrise caves of [Arenfürich](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Geography/Halisan.md#Arenfürich), famed far and wide for the vibrant colors of light that seem trapped within. Cynics sometimes refer to the stone as “fool’s [Gimyndine](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Gimyndine.md),” as good cuts can resemble high-quality gimyns, but those capable of resonating can tell at a glance that torhith lacks the resonating properties which makes gimyndine so useful.
