## Blackfire Devices

A blackfire device is any instrument crafted with the use of low-grade [gimpowder](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Gimyndine.md#Ashstone) in mind, with highly efficient [Flamescript](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Flamescript.md) engraved into it. Due to the extremely low [gimynfire](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Soulfire.md#Gimynfire) production of [blackstone](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Gimyndine.md#Ashstone)–even when combusted–the flamescript which shapes the functions of these devices must be equally efficient, requiring a highly skilled [flamescribe](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Flamescribe.md). Due to this limitation, [Firekeeping](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Applied%20Phlogistics/Firekeeping.md) in the hands of the common folk is limited mostly to combustion, but many flamescribes and noble houses maintain similar items for convenience or war making, which are documented here.
