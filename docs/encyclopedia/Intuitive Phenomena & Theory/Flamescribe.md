# Flamescribe

Specifically, a [Firekeeper](<Firekeeper.md>) who specializes in the creation and transcription of [engrams](<Engram.md>). When used as a title, often shortened simply to "Scribe" or "Scr." in writing. 

Occasionally, one who studies new applications of [Firekeeping](<Firekeeping.md>). (overlap with [Phlogistician](<Phlogistician.md>))
