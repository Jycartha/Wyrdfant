### Infusion

Perhaps the most basic interaction, [Soulfire](<Soulfire.md>) undergoes infusion when it’s radially compressed by [the Conscious Plane](<Conscious Plane.md>), shunting it down into the physical realm. This process also generates a form of radiation called [sparks](<Soulfire.md#Sparks>), in order to preserve the information lost in converting continuous soulfire to quantized reality. Only infused soulfire is capable of directly interacting with matter or the physical forces.
