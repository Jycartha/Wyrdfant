### Spin Friction

Similar to particle physics, the “spin” of [soulfire](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Soulfire.md) refers to, in a sense, its angular momentum. Spin is imparted upon [infused](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Soulfire/Interactions/Infusion.md) soulfire through the addition of a twisting force to radial compression, and higher spin soulfire has the property of a higher “static friction” when infused. That is, it “sticks” better before an applied [conscious force](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/Intention%20Field.md) detaches it. The main limitation of many kinematic systems involving [phlogistics](/The%20Aliffrüme%20Encyclopedia/Intuitive%20Phenomena%20&%20Theory/Pure%20Phlogistics/The%20Phlogistic%20Model.md) is the magnitude of this spin friction, as it limits the upper bound of force that can be applied reliably, thus limiting the weight of manipulable objects.
