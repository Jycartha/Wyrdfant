# Firekeeper  

One who practices [Firekeeping](<Firekeeping.md>), including the creation of [Engram](<Engram.md>) or their implementation, medicine through assisted assertion, or any other such broad fields. Shortened to "Kpr." in writing.

## Gimynkeeper

A [Firekeeper](<Firekeeper.md>) who primarily utilizes a gimyn as their source of intention.
