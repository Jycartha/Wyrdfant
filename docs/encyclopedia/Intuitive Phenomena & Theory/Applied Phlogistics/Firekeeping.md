# Firekeeping

Applied [Phlogistics](<Phlogistics.md>) for various purposes, including engineering, medicine, and many other fields.

---
## Usage

All firekeeping begins with the generation of [Soulfire](<Soulfire.md>), which is then manipulated in some manner to achieve some effect. It can be suddenly compressed to exert an explosive pressure, run over [Flamescript](<Flamescript.md>) to affect [engrams](<Engram.md>), or used for its [fluid mechanics](<The Phlogistic Model.md#Fluidic Pressure>), in increasing order of complexity.

All life can control ambient [Soulfire](<Soulfire.md>) and even produce its own, but sourcing it from [Gimyndine](<Gimyndine.md>) is much more potent and allows easier use when integrated with [Kindlegem](<Kindlegem.md>). This led to [Gimyndine](<Gimyndine.md>) technology and its derivatives becoming the forefront of development for centuries, and many self-sourced firekeeping techniques became relegated to folk contexts or small communities, where the usage of [Gimyndine](<Gimyndine.md>) was either impractical from extenuating circumstance, or rejected on principle.
