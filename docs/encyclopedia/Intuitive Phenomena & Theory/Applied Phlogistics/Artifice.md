### Artifice

The study of combining [Flamescript](<Flamescript.md>) with machinery, giving rise to contraptions capable of [Firekeeping](<Firekeeping.md>). The field is further broken down into fields normally associated with purely physical engineering: Aerial artifice, mechanical artifice, etc. The distinction is practical as not all environments are as conducive to [Gimyndine](<Gimyndine.md>) as others, and some applications simply don’t merit the complexity of phlogistic techniques.
