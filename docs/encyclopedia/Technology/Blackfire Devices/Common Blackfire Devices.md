## Blackfire Devices

A blackfire device is any instrument crafted with the use of low-grade [gimpowder](<Gimyndine.md#Ashstone>) in mind, with highly efficient [Flamescript](<Flamescript.md>) engraved into it. Due to the extremely low [gimynfire](<Soulfire.md#Gimynfire>) production of [blackstone](<Gimyndine.md#Ashstone>)–even when combusted–the flamescript which shapes the functions of these devices must be equally efficient, requiring a highly skilled [flamescribe](<Flamescribe.md>). Due to this limitation, [Firekeeping](<Firekeeping.md>) in the hands of the common folk is limited mostly to combustion, but many flamescribes and noble houses maintain similar items for convenience or war making, which are documented here.
