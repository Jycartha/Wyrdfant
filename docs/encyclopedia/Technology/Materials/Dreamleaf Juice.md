### Dreamleaf Juice

A product drawn from the leaves of dreamleaf plants in the mist-forests of southern [Tohnipras](<Tohnipras.md>), known for its unique psychedelic effect of [immersing](<The Phlogistic Model.md#Immersionists>) users in the thoughts and dreams had by others near the plant before the time of harvesting. This property is believed to be derived from the slight [pneumaphilic](<Pneumaphilic Materials.md>) properties of Tohneasian mist, carrying residual [Soulfire](<Soulfire.md>) from the various creatures it contacts.  

Due to the especially unique quality of offering an exotic perspective, the juice is often vaporized and inhaled as a recreational drug by [Yejian](<Llantakúna.md#Yejian>) aristocrats, considered a high-class treat. The native [Ayauitlaka](<Llantakúna.md#Ayauitlaka>), however, consider the juice sacred as part of local tradition, used only during meditative rituals for the purposes of harmonizing with the forest. Using it for pleasure is considered blasphemous.
