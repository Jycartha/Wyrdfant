### Powderstaff

A common implement for support-focused or civilian [flamebearers](<Flamebearer.md>), and a catch-all term for any device which constructs a [monocrystal](<Adamant.md>) tool. In this case, monocrystal’s use lies within its reusability and relatively high [soulfire](<Soulfire.md>) capacity. Some [engrams](<Engram.md>) require intense bursts of gimynfire, but uncoupled gimynfire disperses quickly, and the human body will succumb to [[glassrot]] rapidly if [coupling](<Coupling.md>) is attempted. As a solution, many tools form monocrystals as a form of quick-discharge capacitor, often resembling staffs or floating orbs.
