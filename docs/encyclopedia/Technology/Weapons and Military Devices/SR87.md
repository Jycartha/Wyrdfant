### SR87

The standard-issue [flamebearer](<Flamebearer.md>)'s scepter of Saorann, which is also able to transform into a sniper rifle through the use of a simple [Engram](<Engram.md>). Can house up to 20 [kindlegems](<Kindlegem.md>), most often long-range support ones. Common engrams include reflective shields, [Gimyndine](<Gimyndine.md>) resonator bombs, aerial bombardment, and trap fields.
